Who We Are

Welcome to Zero Art Curation, a vibrant and innovative art collective rooted in the heart of Manchester. Emerging from the diverse academic landscape of Salford University, our members bring with them backgrounds spanning a multitude of disciplines, including Architecture, Fine Art, Ceramics, Product Design, 3D Art, and Architectural Technologies.

Our collective journey over the past five years has been marked by a commitment to redefining artistic expression, transcending conventional boundaries, and pushing the limits of creativity. Our members hail from various backgrounds, contributing their unique fortes to create a dynamic and multidimensional artistic experience.

Zero Art specializes in a myriad of artistic forms, showcasing a rich tapestry of 2D art, 3D art, mesmerizing GIFs, and captivating .mp4 creations. Our expertise extends across a spectrum of subjects, from the traditional realms of fine and digital art to avant-garde styles such as modern, impressionist, and photorealistic renderings. We proudly immerse ourselves in the world of 3D animations and walkthroughs, crafting experiences that resonate with audiences globally.
